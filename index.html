Board Recommends SUP Requirement

January 13, 2026

The Board of Supervisors unanimously voted to recommend removing by-right data center approval. This means the Planning Commission will now consider requiring Special Use Permits for all data centers.

What's next: Planning Commission reviews in February, then returns to BOS for final vote.

Recent Decision

Technology District Approved

December 16, 2025

The Board of Supervisors approved a new Technology (T) District zoning that allows data centers in Orange County. The vote was 5-0.

Impact: This opened the door to data center development. Community pushback led to the January SUP amendment effort.
