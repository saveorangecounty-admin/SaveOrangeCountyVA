150-acre minimum

500 feet from homes

🌾 Agricultural Setback

300 ft (unimproved) / 500 ft (improved)

🌊 Rapidan River Setback

500 feet minimum

From the river's edge

Closed-loop systems only

No groundwater or Rapidan River water for cooling

65 dBA day / 60 dBA night

Measured at property line

Data centers by-right
